Pebble Game

Fasttouch-3

Explanation

On each turn, you can do one of the following:

  1. Click a leaf (bottom row) node to add a pebble.
  2. Click any pebble to remove it.
  3. If there is a pebble on both nodes below a node, then you can click and hold on a lower pebble to slide it up.
Your goal is to get a pebble to the top of the tree using the smallest possible pebble bank.
